You should watch Jo-Jo's Bizarre adventure

Jo-Jo's bizarre Adventure is an incredible saga of good vs evil. The story follows the multigenerational tale of the Joestar family across different settings, time periods and villains. Yet everything is tied together both strongly and loosely enough to have kept the story flowing with a publishing history starting in 1987 to the present day (2025). The Mangaka, Hirohiko Araki, has cemented his talent as an author and artist with this series in particular, JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, having been exhibited at The Louvre; a rather special claim amongst Mangaka.
